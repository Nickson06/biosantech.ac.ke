About Us

BTTI is a private Technical and Vocational Education Training agency registered in the Republic of Kenya as an International Community Based Health, Development, Research and Training Institution, located in Mamboleo Kisumu City, offering training in Biosanitation Technology; Community Health related courses; Building and construction, plumbing courses. BTTI training involves learners in real life service delivery as workers, to prepare them for the job market.

Biosanitation Technology Training Institute (BTTI) is implementing the Modular Employable Skills (MES) under the Competency Based Education & Training (CBET) Scheme to provide vocational training to earlier school leavers and existing workers to develop skilled manpower for the industry. Under the Modular Employable Skills, training to earlier school leavers and existing workers, especially in the unorganized sectors, are to be trained for employable skills.

We also offer services (construction, installation and servicing(maintenance) of bio sanitation systems to households, communities, schools/institutions, and market places for those in need of the service/facility at affordable costs. We provide optimum and customized solutions tailored to your organization's needs. Our mandate extends to various business areas, including the construction of bio-digesters, general construction, sewerage service management, and environmental impact assessments/audits consultancy, among others.

Frequently Asked Questions

What are the admission requirements for your courses?

Our admission requirements vary depending on the course. Generally, you need a high school diploma or equivalent, a completed application form, and, in some cases, prerequisite coursework or relevant work experience. Specific requirements for each course can be found on the Admissions page.

Are there any financial aid options available?

Yes, we offer various financial aid options including scholarships, grants, and payment plans. Detailed information on financial aid opportunities and how to apply can be found on our Financial Aid page.

What career support services do you provide?

We provide a range of career support services including resume workshops, interview preparation, job search assistance, and connections with industry professionals. Our Career Services team is dedicated to helping you succeed in your chosen field.
